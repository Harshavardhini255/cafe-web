Our Story

From Bean to Cup,
a Labor of Love

Founded with a passion for great coffee and good company, WakeCup Cafe in Theni is a cozy corner where every visit feels like home. Managed by Anjana, we serve specialty coffee, handcrafted sandwiches, and indulgent desserts made with love.

Located in NRT Nagar, our space is designed for connection — whether you're catching up with friends, hosting a celebration, or joining our creative workshops.
